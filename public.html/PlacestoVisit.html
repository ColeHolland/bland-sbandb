Bland's Bed and Breakfast has some very interesting places to visit around one of our main locations in Indianola, Iowa. One of the main things to do when you visit Bland's is to schedule a visit around the Indianola Balloon Classic. The Balloon Classic brings thousands of people to the city of Indianola each summer, and displays a great amount of colorful balloons that cover the evening and night sky. Each year Bland's has our very own hot air balloon that we fly in the classic. To see which balloon is ours, just look for the beige writing!

Along with the short lived Balloon Classic, Bland's is also located close to the capital city of Iowa, Des Moines. With Indianola only being a short 20-30 minute drive away from Des Moines, families can easily take a trip to the capital city to go shopping, sightseeing, or simply have a night on the town.

Finally, Bland's is conveniently located next to Simpson College. Simpson College is a private college located right smack dab in the middle of the city of Indianola. With attendance not being as high as some of the other public colleges in the state, Simpson can provide a much greater experience to its students and as well as the community around.
